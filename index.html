The Endless Barrage

If you want to push in Overwatch 2 this is the teamcomp to play.

  • Tank - Orisa
  • DPS - Pharah
  • DPS - Junkrat
  • DPS - Hanzo
  • Healer - Mercy

This is a hyper aggressive comp that consists of 3 DPS units which yields high damage. It also utilizes Pharmacy the Pharah, Mercy combo that was popular in the orignal Overwatch.

Tank - Orisa

Orisa, a tank hero in Overwatch, is an omnic peacekeeper designed to safeguard Numbani. Created by the prodigious engineer Efi Oladele, Orisa is known for her protective nature and commitment to global security. Her Fusion Driver, a rapid-fire machine gun, offers substantial damage output and control over enemy movements. Fortify, her defensive ability, reduces damage taken and grants immunity to crowd control effects, making her a resilient frontline presence. Orisa's Halt! ability unleashes a graviton charge, disrupting enemy formations and facilitating coordinated attacks. Additionally, her Protective Barrier provides reliable cover for her team, enabling strategic positioning and objective defense. When the situation demands a power boost, Orisa's ultimate ability, Supercharger, amplifies the damage output of nearby allies, turning the tide of battle in their favor. In essence, Orisa excels as a steadfast protector, combining defensive prowess with offensive capabilities to bolster her team's success on the battlefield.

DPS - Pharah

Pharah, a damage hero in Overwatch, is a highly mobile offense character renowned for her aerial superiority and devastating firepower. As a member of the elite Helix Security International, Pharah, also known as Fareeha Amari, wields a versatile rocket launcher as her primary weapon, dealing explosive damage from afar. Her Jump Jet ability allows her to soar into the air, granting her superior positioning and vantage points over the battlefield. With her Concussive Blast ability, Pharah can propel herself or her enemies, disrupting enemy formations and securing strategic advantages. Additionally, she possesses the Hover Jets ability, enabling her to sustain flight and rain down barrages of rockets upon her foes. Pharah's ultimate ability, Barrage, unleashes a relentless barrage of rockets, dealing massive area-of-effect damage and decimating enemy defenses. Overall, Pharah excels as a high-flying aerial threat, dominating the skies and delivering devastating blows to her adversaries with precision and style.

DPS - Junkrat

Junkrat, a damage hero in Overwatch, is a chaotic and explosive force on the battlefield, known for his unorthodox methods and gleeful mayhem. A former Junker and demolitionist from the irradiated wastelands of Australia, Junkrat revels in chaos and destruction. Armed with a Frag Launcher, he lobs grenades at his enemies, dealing area-of-effect damage with each explosive projectile. Junkrat's Concussion Mine ability allows him to propel himself or his adversaries through the air, granting him unexpected mobility and disruptive potential. He also employs Steel Traps, which immobilize and damage enemies who trigger them, effectively controlling choke points and denying enemy movement. Additionally, Junkrat's Rip-Tire ultimate unleashes a remote-controlled tire packed with explosives, capable of devastating entire enemy teams with a single detonation. In essence, Junkrat embodies controlled chaos, utilizing his explosive arsenal and unpredictable maneuvers to sow confusion and wreak havoc upon his foes with reckless abandon.

DPS - Hanzo

Hanzo, a damage hero in Overwatch, embodies precision and deadly accuracy as a skilled archer and assassin. Descended from a long line of Japanese warriors, Hanzo is a master of the bow, wielding his Storm Bow with unmatched skill and finesse. His arrows strike with pinpoint accuracy, dealing high damage and capable of headshots for increased lethality. Hanzo's Sonic Arrow ability allows him to reveal enemy positions through walls, providing invaluable reconnaissance for his team and enabling strategic plays. With his Lunge ability, Hanzo can swiftly reposition himself, granting him agility and evasiveness in combat situations. Furthermore, his ultimate ability, Dragonstrike, summons twin spirit dragons that carve through terrain and enemies alike, dealing devastating damage in their path. Hanzo excels as a versatile damage dealer, capable of controlling sightlines, picking off key targets, and turning the tide of battle with his deadly precision and powerful abilities.

Healer - Mercy

Mercy, a support hero in Overwatch, epitomizes compassion and healing, serving as a guardian angel to her teammates in the heat of battle. As a dedicated field medic and member of the Overwatch organization, Mercy is armed with a Caduceus Staff, allowing her to heal allies with a steady stream of health or boost their damage output with the Caduceus Blaster. Her Guardian Angel ability enables Mercy to quickly fly to the aid of wounded allies or escape dangerous situations, providing crucial mobility and positioning. Additionally, she possesses the Resurrect ability, which allows her to bring fallen teammates back into the fight, turning the tide of battle with a single act of revival. Mercy's ultimate ability, Valkyrie, enhances her powers, granting her increased mobility, healing, and damage boost capabilities, empowering her to support her team with even greater efficiency. In essence, Mercy embodies selflessness and dedication, ensuring the survival and success of her allies through unwavering support and healing prowess.
